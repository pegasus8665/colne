COMPREHENSIVE LENDING SOLUTIONS

We architect bespoke financing structures that traditional retail banks simply cannot offer directly.

Unlock the highest Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratios available in the market—up to 80% for UAE Nationals and 80% for Expats on their first property. We negotiate aggressively to secure the lowest fixed interest rates to shield you from fluctuating EIBOR, ensuring your monthly payments remain affordable and predictable.

We specialize in sourcing financing for investors living outside the UAE. Relying on foreign income, tax returns, and international credit reports, we can secure up to 60-80% financing for your Dubai investment properties with remarkably streamlined documentation.

Scale your business footprint with dedicated lending facilities for warehouses, office floors, retail spaces, and staff accommodations. We structure commercial loans tailored to your company's audited financials and cash-flow projections with amortizations up to 15 years.

Trapped in an expensive variable rate? We facilitate seamless mortgage transfers between banks (buyouts) to lock in lower interest rates. We also specialize in Equity Release, allowing you to unlock the capital tied up in your existing properties to fund new investments or business ventures.
